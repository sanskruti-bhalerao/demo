Social Media

Social media refers to online platforms that enable users to communicate, share content, and build communities. It encompasses various forms like social networking sites, blogs, and video-sharing platforms. Social media's impact is widespread, affecting communication, marketing, and even news consumption

Types of Social Media:

    Social Networking site

  1. Platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn where users connect with friends, family, and colleagues, sharing updates, photos, and videos.

    2. MicroBlogging

  2. Platforms like Twitter (now X) allow for short, quick updates and conversations.

    3. Video sharing

  3. Websites like YouTube and TikTok enable users to upload, share, and watch videos

    4. Photo Sharing

  4. Platforms like Instagram and Pinterest focus on visual content, allowing users to share photos and discover inspiration.

    5. Professional Paring

  5. LinkedIn is a platform for professionals to connect, network, and find job opportunities

    6. Messaging Apps

  6. Apps like WhatsApp and Messenger facilitate direct communication between users.
